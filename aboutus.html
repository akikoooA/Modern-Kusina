About Modern Kusina

Bringing Global Flavors To Your Kitchen

Our Story

Modern Kusina began with a simple idea to make international cuisine accessible to home cooks everywhere.
Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate food enthusiasts, our platform has grown into a community of culinary
explorers sharing authentic recipes from around the world.

The word "Kusina" comes from the Filipino word for "kitchen" - a place where families gather, traditions are
passed down, and memories are made. We believe that food is a universal language that connects cultures and
brings people together.

Our mission is to inspire home cooks to step out of their comfort zones and experience the rich diversity of global
cuisine. We strive to provide reliable, tested recipes that honor traditional techniques while adapting them for
the modern kitchen.

Our Mission

To make international cuisine accessible to everyone by providing authentic, easy-to-follow recipes that celebrate cultural diversity through food.

Our Vision

A world where cooking becomes a journey of cultural exploration, bringing people together through shared culinary experiences.
