Quick & Easy Meal Hack: Chicken Pastil

Chicken Pastil is a budget-friendly, filling, and delicious Filipino rice meal. It features flavorful shredded chicken sautéed with spices, served over steamed rice, and topped with a savory sauce.

Why it's a life hack: It’s cheap, easy to prepare in bulk, and perfect for busy days or packed lunches. Just wrap it in banana leaves or foil for a grab-and-go meal!
